## Research Procedure for Policy/SOP Engagements

For each new policy/SOP, research in this order:

1. **Jurisdiction identification** — confirm all jurisdictions the client operates in and which regulators apply
2. **Regulatory scan** — check applicable instruments from the table above (use `regulatory_lookup.py`, not general search)
3. **Recent regulatory updates** — search regulator websites for circulars or guidance issued in the past 12 months
4. **Industry-specific requirements** — some sectors (banking, insurance, listed entities) have additional obligations beyond general labour/corporate law
5. **Peer benchmark** (optional) — for large clients, general search for comparable firm policies in the same industry/jurisdiction

**Source trust hierarchy (applies at deliverable stage):**
- Final deliverable cites only `authoritative` sources from this list
- `reference` sources (ACFE, ISO, IIA) are cited as professional standards — note subscription basis
- `general` web sources are used for research only, not cited in final deliverable
