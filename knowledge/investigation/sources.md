## General Research (can be cited as "general" — not authoritative)

- Tavily general web search
- Industry fraud typology reports
- News and press sources (for background context only)
- ACFE Report to the Nations (fraud benchmarks)

## Investigation-Type Specific Sources

### Asset Misappropriation
- Procurement fraud: vendor registries, invoice analysis standards
- Payroll fraud: HR control frameworks

### AML / Financial Crime
- FATF (fatf-gafi.org) — global AML/CFT standards
- MENA FATF — regional AML context
- Egmont Group — financial intelligence units

### Cybercrime / Digital Fraud
- UAE Cybercrime Law
- DFSA cybersecurity guidance
- ADGM technology and cybersecurity rules

## Research Sequence per Investigation Case

1. Screen all named individuals and entities: OFAC + UN + EU sanctions
2. Look up company ownership for involved entities
3. Pull regulatory requirements for case type + client's regulator
4. Search industry fraud typologies for the case type
5. Check for any prior public incidents involving named parties
