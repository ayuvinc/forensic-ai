# FRM Research Sources

## Authoritative Regulatory Sources

| Source | URL | What it covers |
|--------|-----|----------------|
| CBUAE | cb.gov.ae | UAE Central Bank regulations, Chapter 11 AML/CFT |
| DFSA | dfsa.ae | Dubai Financial Services Authority rules |
| ADGM / FSRA | adgm.com, fsra.ae | Abu Dhabi Global Market financial services rules |
| SCA | sca.gov.ae | Securities and Commodities Authority regulations |

## Research Databases (General)

- Tavily general search — for industry fraud typologies, benchmarks, news
- Zawya — UAE company registries and financials
- UAE company registries — official ownership and corporate filings

## Topics to Research Per Engagement

For each new FRM client, research:
1. `[industry] fraud typologies UAE` — sector-specific fraud patterns
2. `[industry] [regulator] AML requirements` — applicable regulatory rules
3. `[company name]` — official registry, ownership, any public incidents
4. `sanctions [key individuals]` — OFAC + UN + EU screening
5. `[industry] fraud risk register benchmark` — what peer companies typically cover
