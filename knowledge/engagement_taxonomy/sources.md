## ACFE (Reference — member access)

| Source | Access | What it covers |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| ACFE Fraud Examiners Manual | acfe.com (member) | Comprehensive fraud investigation methodology; scheme classification; evidence standards; interview procedures |
| ACFE Report to the Nations (annual) | acfe.com/report | Fraud typology prevalence by region and industry; median loss data; control effectiveness benchmarks |
| ACFE Practice Guides (by topic) | acfe.com (member) | Topic-specific guides: procurement fraud, payroll fraud, financial statement fraud, etc. |

## Industry Fraud Typology Research

For each new engagement, research fraud typology prevalence in the client's industry and jurisdiction:

1. **ACFE Report to the Nations** — current edition; filter by industry and region
2. **CBUAE / DFSA circulars** — recent regulatory guidance on emerging fraud types for regulated entities
3. **General search** (Tavily — general_search.py) — recent news on fraud in the client's industry/jurisdiction
4. **Transparency International** (transparency.org) — country-level corruption perception; useful for ABC and bribery risk context
