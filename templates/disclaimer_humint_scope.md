# ARCH-GAP-02 — HUMINT Scope Flag

**Usage:** Inject into Due Diligence deliverables when Phase 2 (Enhanced DD) or any
scope component requiring discreet source enquiries is selected at intake.

---

## Standard Text (English)

> **HUMINT Scope — Manual Execution Required**
>
> This engagement scope includes components that require discreet human source enquiries (HUMINT). HUMINT activities — including source identification, contact, and intelligence elicitation — cannot be performed by automated tools or AI-assisted research.
>
> The section(s) below define the HUMINT scope, objectives, and information requirements for this engagement. Execution of these activities requires deployment of qualified human investigators with appropriate jurisdictional access and authorisation.
>
> **This tool has defined the scope; delivery of HUMINT findings is a manual step** to be completed by the assigned investigator before the final report can be issued. Output from HUMINT activities should be uploaded to the case folder and integrated into the final deliverable during the review stage.

---

## Short Form (for section headers)

> *⚠ HUMINT Required — This section requires manual investigator action. See scope definition below.*

---

## Injection Points

- Due Diligence reports: insert as a callout box at the start of any Phase 2 / Enhanced DD section
- Investigation Report: insert if scope includes source-based intelligence components
- Do NOT inject in: FRM, Policy/SOP, Transaction Testing, Sanctions Screening (unless explicitly scoped)

---

## Trigger Conditions

Inject ARCH-GAP-02 when any of the following are selected at intake:
- DD Phase: "Enhanced" or "Phase 2"
- Scope component includes: "source enquiries", "HUMINT", "discreet enquiries", "human intelligence"
- Investigation type: includes reputational intelligence or lifestyle enquiries
