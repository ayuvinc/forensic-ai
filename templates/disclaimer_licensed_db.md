# ARCH-GAP-01 — Licensed Database Disclaimer

**Usage:** Inject into all Due Diligence and Sanctions Screening deliverables automatically.
Insert after the Methodology section and again in the Conclusion/Limitations section.

---

## Standard Text (English)

> **Screening Scope Limitation**
>
> This screening was conducted using publicly available official sanctions and regulatory lists, including: OFAC Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) List, UN Security Council Consolidated Sanctions List, EU Consolidated Financial Sanctions List, UK Office of Financial Sanctions Implementation (OFSI) List, and UAE Cabinet Resolution Sanctions List.
>
> This screening does **not** include commercial database screening (e.g., WorldCheck, WorldCompliance, Dow Jones Risk & Compliance, LexisNexis Diligence, or equivalent services). Commercial databases aggregate additional adverse media, PEP data, and proprietary risk signals not accessible through public lists alone.
>
> For acquisition-grade, regulatory-grade, or high-risk counterparty due diligence, supplementary commercial database screening is recommended. GoodWork can facilitate commercial database screening as an additional engagement component upon request.

---

## Short Form (for memo headers)

> *Screening limited to public official lists (OFAC, UN, EU, UK OFSI, UAE). Commercial database screening not included. See Methodology for full scope.*

---

## Injection Points

- Due Diligence reports: after Methodology section header; repeated in Limitations subsection of Conclusion
- Sanctions Screening memos: after Scope section; in footer of clearance memo
- Transaction Testing reports: not required unless sanctions exposure is a test objective
